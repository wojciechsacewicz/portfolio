# Portfolio inspiration direction

## Primary reference

- SOHub Digital: cinematic, project-first structure, short copy, strong transitions and large media without turning the founder portrait into the main asset.

## Supporting references

- darkroom.engineering: compact work index, activity log and creative-development motion.
- Szymon Dziukiewicz: restrained use of WebGL and 3D as a signature rather than decoration everywhere.
- Metajive: featured work before agency claims and concise project framing.
- Pixelmatters 10 Years: immersive transitions and timeline storytelling; useful as an interaction reference, not as a layout to copy wholesale.
- Ashpdeep: direct personal positioning and clear contact path.

## Rules for this portfolio

- Keep the original intro and contribution graph as recognizable personal signatures.
- Keep the FAQ crawlable, compact and visually secondary.
- Use projects as the main visual assets; do not use a large portrait on the homepage.
- Avoid generic AI manifest copy, repeated card grids, decorative pills and oversized slogans.
- Use motion to connect sections and media, not to delay access to content.
- Preserve real project links, measured outcomes and current work context.
