## Evidence standard

External references should support one or more verifiable facts:

- a live product exists;
- a public repository or contribution exists;
- a formal role or collaboration exists;
- a measured outcome is scoped and explained;
- the named technologies are visible in the implementation or project documentation.

The goal is a coherent evidence graph, not repetition for its own sake.
